About Yixuan Li

CV


I am Yixuan Li (李, 翊瑄) from Tianjin, China. I'm now in both Santa Barbara and Bay Area, CA, USA.

I do research in data visualization, technologies for novice users, digital humanities, and so on...

I create things with my body and represent it with visualization and audio.

I want to explore how technologies can shape people's bebavior and promote people's understanding.

I would like to trace back to the early history to understand where I'm from and what I represent.
